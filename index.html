Cardiology

Dedicated to the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of heart and vascular diseases. Our cardiology department offers comprehensive care through advanced diagnostic testing, personalized treatment plans, and state-of-the-art procedures to ensure optimal heart health for our patients.

Neurology

Specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system. Our neurology department provides expert care for conditions such as epilepsy, stroke, multiple sclerosis, and neurodegenerative diseases, utilizing advanced diagnostic tools and individualized treatment strategies to enhance the quality of life for our patients.

Hepatology

Focused on the comprehensive care of liver diseases, our hepatology department specializes in diagnosing and treating conditions such as hepatitis, fatty liver disease, cirrhosis, and liver cancer. We provide personalized treatment plans, advanced diagnostic techniques, and access to cutting-edge therapies to support optimal liver health and overall well-being.

Pediatrics

Committed to the health and well-being of infants, children, and adolescents, our pediatrics department offers comprehensive medical care tailored to the unique needs of young patients. We provide preventive care, vaccinations, and treatment for acute and chronic illnesses, ensuring a supportive and nurturing environment for children and their families.

Eye Care

Dedicated to preserving and enhancing vision, our Eye Care department provides comprehensive services for all age groups. We offer routine eye exams, advanced diagnostic testing, and specialized treatments for conditions such as glaucoma, cataracts, and refractive errors. Our team of experienced ophthalmologists and optometrists is committed to delivering personalized care to help you achieve optimal eye health.
